Project Overview

This system was developed with the aim of providing a simple and efficient solution for small businesses looking to migrate from spreadsheets to a computerized system. Utilizing CustomTkinter for the graphical interface and SQLite database for data storage, the system offers various functionalities to assist in the day-to-day management of the business.

The aim of this system is to provide an affordable and user-friendly alternative for companies seeking to automate processes and organize their daily operations. With an intuitive interface and essential functionalities, this system can assist small businesses in increasing their efficiency and productivity.

Main Features:

Home: View the main dashboard with quick access to key functionalities.
Inventory: Inventory control with features to add, edit, and delete products.
Registration: Management of customer, supplier, and other business partner registrations, as well as their products.
Agenda: Integrated calendar for scheduling appointments, meetings, and important events.
Wallet: Basic financial control to monitor income and expenses.
Finance: Advanced functionalities for financial analysis and monitoring.
User: Allows managing users and system access permissions.
Settings: Customization of system settings according to the specific needs of the company.
