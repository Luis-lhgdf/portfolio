Ztype Bot

Bot utilizing image and text recognition to aid in the stages of game ZType.

Live Link

Project Overview

This project utilizes CustomTkinter for the graphical interface and Pytesseract for image recognition, aiming to enhance knowledge in image recognition techniques. The bot is designed to analyze the gameplay screen of ZType, identifying text elements such as words or phrases that need to be typed. By leveraging Pytesseract, it extracts textual data from screenshots, allowing the bot to determine the words to type. With this setup, the bot can aid players by recognizing incoming words or phrases in the game, providing assistance or automation in typing them out accurately and swiftly. Through this project, the aim is not only to create a functional bot but also to delve deeper into the intricacies of image recognition, understanding its applications and limitations in practical scenarios like gaming environments.

Tools Used

Python
CustomTkinter
Pytesseract
